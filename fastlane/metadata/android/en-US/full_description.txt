<p><i>SoundCrowd</i> is a free, open-source and lightweight music player for Android in modern material design, specialized for playing long music tracks (DJ mixes, live sets, audio books).</p><p>It features the generation of waveforms that visualize your music tracks during playback and can be used for precise seeking through gestures.</p><p>You can create cue points at your favorite positions in your music tracks. With these markers in the waveform, you can remember them and easily jump back to them.</p><p>Want to know the track id of a specific part in a mix? SoundCrowd comes with build-in audio tagging support by using SongRec, an open-source Shazam client implementation, and creates cue points for found track ids. Unlike Shazam, the app records the internal audio of the player, so you don't need to grant access to the microphone or play the music loud!</p><p>The app contains build-in plugin modules to support the following online streaming services:<ul><li>SoundCloud<li>YouTube</li><li>Spotify</li><li>Beatport</li><li>Tidal</li></ul></p>
